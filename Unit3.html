Unit 3 : Collecting Data

Important Concepts

  • Types of Sampling
    • Stratified random- unbiased, independent, and all samples of size n have the same probability of being selected
    • Cluster- population is grouped into heterogeneous clusters
    • Systematic- population is grouped into homogenous groups
  • Types of Experiments
    • Completely randomized- the experimental units are assigned to the treatments completely by chance
    • Randomized block- sample is split into groups who share similar traits before treatments
    • Matched pairs- type of blocking with just two treatments
  • Types of Bias
    • Undercoverage- not everyone is adequately represented
    • Non-response- not everyone responds
    • Response- people feel pressured to give socially acceptable responses
    • Wording- wording of question pressures a certain answer
    • Voluntary- only those extremely passionate will respond
